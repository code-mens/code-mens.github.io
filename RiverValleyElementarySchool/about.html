ABOUT US

River Valley Elementary School is a private elementary school in Ghana for boys and girls aged 3 to 12 located at Dansoman, Accra. Our motto is excellence through hard work and our vision is to produce god-fearing reliable and patriotic children. Inside our blue gate we offer a wonderful environment for children to learn, play and develop their knowledge and talents. Some of our standard core subjects include mathematics, science, language arts, reading comprehension, geography and social studies. Other optional subjects include computers, music, art and basic foreign languages such as Spanish, French or German.

OUR VISION

We believe that through the schools core values of hardwork, determination, respect and reliability we can produce well-learned, God-fearing and patriotic citizens who would contribute greatly to development in all areas.

OUR MISSION

Through hard work and determination we aim at making our students excel in all spheres of life by providing the highest quality of education to them. We also aim at developing the skill set and talent to bring out the great potential in our students.

CORE VALUES

The school is built on these four principles and by these principles we keep moving forward.

  • Hardwork

    To achieve success we need a great deal of effort and endurance.

  • Determination

    Achieving any set goal would be futile without firmness of purpose.

  • Respect

    Respect for ourselves guides our morals, but respect for other guides our manners.

  • Reliability

    Reliability is the fuel for compounding strength of a leader.
